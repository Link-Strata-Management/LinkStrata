Your Boutique Strata Managers

LINK Corporate Services Pty Ltd trading as LINK Strata Management (LINK) was incorporated in 1998 to provide administration and management support to local organisations. The company is based on an accounting practice and provides prompt and efficient service from highly skilled and trained staff.
LINK recognises the increasingly complex demands and time constraints placed on members of Executive Committees and the desire for a solution to the issues that typically concern the owners and Committee Members of Owners Corporations.
LINK assists Owners Corporations by providing access to timely and professional administration, management, maintenance and compliance resources in a cost effective structure. LINK will relieve Committee Members of many of the frustrations they currently face, in order that they may more fully enjoy the benefits of property ownership.

Our Services

Professional Financial Management

LINK’s financial management leads to a transparency of figures and supports a real understanding of the numbers in your Unit Title. We simply allow you to sleep much easier at night knowing you're in safe hands.

Insurance

LINK keeps your property insured in line with current legislation. We work with your chosen insurance company to manage claims, valuations, quotations and insurance renewals. LINK collaborates with owners to optimize insurance costs.

Maintenance & Repairs

Prompt action on any maintenance is crucial to protecting the value of your complex, and we treat it with absolute priority. Communication and transparency are the key in any working relationship. We arrange repairs and maintenance, including quotes, so we make sure everything is done professionally and cost-effectively.

Administration Management

LINK's expert team values the importance of accurate record keeping. Our processes have been established to ensure high accuracy and detail. LINK is a member of the Strata Community Association, providing education, accreditation, advice, and advocacy to enable greater understanding of the legislative environment.

Tailored Porfolio Management

LINK understands every customers needs are different and expectations are ever-changing. LINK's focused team is proactive in developing solutions for your requirements.

Governance

LINK’s devoted team will make certain your Units Plan complies with the Unit Titles (Management) Act 2011 LINK will assist with briefing solicitors; drafting, amending and reviewing the rules; renewing crown leases and draft and issue notices against owners and tenants for infringing the rules.

The LINK Approach

Effective Communication

Effective communication helps us better understand a person or situation and enables us to resolve differences, build trust and respect, and create environments where creative ideas and problem solving can flourish.

Dedication

Our approach is to have dedicated staff that can respond according to your requirements. LINK operates with a lower than industry average of properties per Strata Manager to administrate your property more effectively and provide you with a higher quality of service.

Flexibility

We have well defined processes and preferred methods for executing our Strata Management Services. Every Units Plan is unique, we would love to chat about tailoring our services to meet your unique and specific requirements.
